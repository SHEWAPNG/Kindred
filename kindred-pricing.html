Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. No contracts, no lock-ins. Cancel anytime and you keep access until the end of your billing period.

Do you store my inputs? +

No. Your thoughts are private. We never store your inputs or use them to train our models.

What counts as a transformation? +

Each time you submit an input and receive an output. Regenerating also counts as one.

Can I pay in dollars? +

Yes! Toggle to USD above. We accept both Naira and USD payments securely through Paystack.

Is there a student discount? +

Yes! Students with a valid .edu email get 50% off Pro. Sign up with your student email to claim it automatically.
